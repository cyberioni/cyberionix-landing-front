Master Data Management
  • CMDM (Cyberionix Master Data Management)
    – platform for effective dimensional information management.
    CMDM allows:
  • Describe dimensions structure
  • Using dimensions while cleansing and validating data
  • Store dimensional data
  • Provide dimensional data to external systems
  • Search, review and edit dimensional data
    CMDM is a foundation for creating effective business processes and enables to:
  • Automate collaboration of information sub-systems of the organization which use dimensional information;
  • Conform dimensional information across all company branches;
  • Enable role based access to dimensional information;
  • Eliminate duplicates and ambiguity, ensure referential integrity and conformity of dimensional data across all levels within organization.
