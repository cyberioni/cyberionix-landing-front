COMPANY OVERVIEW

Cyberionix is innovative, open-source oriented BI and Analytical services company that provides client-taylored BI solutions. With a unique blend of market knowledge and technology expertise, we provide range of services that help clients attain their strategic objectives of enhanced efficiency, revenue growth, and cost reduction. Our services can be grouped into the following broad categories: Predictive Analytics, Data Mining, Business Research, and Data Warehousing and BI.
